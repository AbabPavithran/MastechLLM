Our Process

How We Work

Every project follows a proven methodology — from initial survey and engineering design through fabrication, installation, and final commissioning. We don't cut corners; we cut steel.

01
Step 01
Consultation & Survey

We begin with a thorough site visit and needs assessment. Your project brief drives our planning — no assumptions, only verified data and requirements.

02
Step 02
Engineering & Design

Our engineering team produces detailed shop drawings and structural calculations reviewed and approved before any fabrication or site work commences.

03
Step 03
Fabrication & QC

In-house fabrication under strict quality control by ASME IX qualified welders. Every weld, joint, and component is inspected against international standards.

04
Step 04
Installation & Commissioning

Expert site erection and installation with minimal disruption, followed by full commissioning, documentation, and client sign-off. Every project delivered right.
