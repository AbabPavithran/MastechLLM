Methodology

breakthrough.

From first engineering consultation through final commissioning — a proven four-stage methodology that eliminates risk at every phase.

01

Discovery

Deep-dive engineering consultation, site assessment, and requirement mapping against project constraints.

02

Design & FEA

3D LiDAR scanning, finite element analysis, and full digital twin simulation before a single weld is made.

03

Fabrication

04

Delivery & QA

NDT testing, third-party inspection, on-site commissioning, and 24-month warranty coverage.

01
Discovery & Engineering

We begin with detailed client briefs and site surveys, assigning a dedicated engineering team to translate requirements into code-compliant designs with full FEA validation.

Site Survey FEA Analysis Code Review 3D Modelling
02
Material Procurement

Certified mill-tested materials sourced from approved vendors. Full traceability with MTCs, heat numbers, and hardness certifications maintained throughout.

Mill Certs Traceability Vendor Audit
03
Fabrication & Welding

In-house fabrication by certified welders using qualified WPS/PQR procedures. Dimensional checks at every stage with final fit-up review by lead inspector.

WPS/PQR PWHT Dimensional QC
04
Inspection & Delivery

Third-party NDT, hydrostatic testing, and final documentation package before supervised loading and delivery with full as-built dossier.

UT/RT/MPI Hydrotest As-built Docs
