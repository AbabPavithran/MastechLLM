DISCOVER THE WORKFLOW
Our Workflow

Precision at every
phase.

From initial consultation to final handover — each stage is governed by rigorous quality control, digital traceability, and continuous client collaboration.

01

Discovery & Consultation

Deep-dive engineering assessment

Our process begins with an intensive discovery phase. We analyze project requirements, site conditions, regulatory constraints, and performance targets. This phase includes stakeholder alignment, feasibility studies, and preliminary risk assessment.

  • Site survey & 3D LiDAR scanning
  • Requirements engineering workshop
  • Preliminary budget & schedule
  • Regulatory compliance mapping
02

Design & FEA Simulation

Digital twin before physical fabrication

Using advanced CAD software and finite element analysis (FEA), our engineers create detailed 3D models and simulate real-world stresses, thermal loads, and fatigue cycles — eliminating design flaws before production begins.

  • 3D parametric modeling (SolidWorks / AutoCAD)
  • FEA structural & thermal analysis (ANSYS)
  • Digital twin generation
  • Client design review & sign-off
03

Fabrication & Assembly

Precision manufacturing at scale

Certified welders (ASME IX, AWS D1.1) and CNC operators execute production using robotic welding cells, automated cutting tables, and real‑time quality monitoring. Every component is serialized for full traceability.

  • Robotic MIG/TIG/SAW welding
  • CNC plasma & laser cutting (±0.2mm)
  • In-process dimensional inspection
  • Heat treatment & surface finishing
04

Quality Control & NDT

Zero-defect verification

All fabricated components undergo rigorous non‑destructive testing (NDT) including ultrasonic, radiographic, magnetic particle, and dye penetrant inspection — performed by ASNT Level III certified technicians.

  • UT / RT / MT / PT inspections
  • Third-party witness & certification
  • Pressure testing (hydrostatic/pneumatic)
  • Material verification (PMI / hardness)
05

Site Erection & Installation

On‑ground execution excellence

Our certified site crews execute structural assembly, piping installation, and equipment setting with strict adherence to HSE protocols. Real‑time progress tracking via client portal ensures full transparency.

  • Heavy lifting & rigging plans
  • Pre‑assembly & modularization
  • On‑site welding & fit‑up
  • Daily HSE toolbox talks & audits
06

Commissioning & Handover

Operational readiness + training

Full system testing, performance validation, and client training. We deliver complete as‑built documentation, warranties, and a 24‑month after‑sales support plan.

  • System integration testing (SIT)
  • Performance & load testing
  • Operator & maintenance training
  • Final documentation & warranty package
