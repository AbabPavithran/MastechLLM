Our Legacy

Engineered Excellence
Since 2007

Founded in 2007 under Umm Al Quwain License No. 4840, Mastech Technical Iron Works L.L.C has grown into a diversified engineering powerhouse, specializing in Mechanical Engineering, Steel Fabrication, Manufacturing, Plant Maintenance, Oil Field Services, and Equipment Manufacturing.

Our 17+ years of hands-on industrial experience enable us to deliver complex projects across oil & gas, petrochemical, infrastructure, and offshore sectors — always with precision, safety, and a relentless pursuit of perfection.
