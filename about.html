Our Identity
"Precision is not a target — it is the only acceptable standard."

Since 2007, Mastech Technical Iron Works has delivered high-stakes industrial fabrication and engineering services across the UAE and beyond. What began as a 12-person vision in Umm Al Quwain has grown into a 215-engineer force — ISO 9001 certified, ASME Section VIII compliant, and API Q1 registered.

We combine old-world craftsmanship with cutting-edge digital manufacturing: robotic welding cells, 3D LiDAR scanning, finite element analysis, and AI-assisted design — all under one roof, all built for accountability.

The Story

Built on
steel and
conviction.

2007

The Foundation

A team of 12 engineers with a shared belief that UAE's industrial future demanded a new benchmark in fabrication quality. Mastech was founded in Umm Al Quwain with a single fabrication unit and an uncompromising tolerance for mediocrity.

2012

The Breakthrough

A landmark ADNOC contract for offshore platform steel structures — 3,200 tons delivered on schedule. This project established Mastech as a tier-one industrial supplier in the UAE energy sector.

2020

The Transformation

AI-driven design, robotic welding cells, and 3D LiDAR scanning rolled out across all facilities. Digital manufacturing meets old-world craftsmanship — a combination no competitor has replicated.

2025

The Future

Carbon-neutral facility commissioned. Dedicated R&D lab and materials science division opened. Mastech enters its next phase — sustainable precision at industrial scale.
